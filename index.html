What the DDFA Does

The DDFA establishes guardrails for corporate data practices, bans harmful profiling, enforces strong security standards, and requires transparent accounting when data generates revenue.

Why It Matters

Minnesotans deserve digital self-determination. The DDFA restores trust, protects vulnerable communities, reduces exploitation, and ensures that individuals benefit from the value their data creates.
