THE DDFA — Data Distribution Fairness Act

The DDFA establishes a Digital Bill of Rights, regulates the monetization of personal data, ensures fair compensation, transparency, and protects Minnesotans against harmful profiling.

A Digital Rights Movement for Minnesotans.

Digital Bill of Rights

  • Right to Transparency
  • Right to Access, Correction, and Deletion
  • Right to Consent and Opt-In Monetization
  • Right to Portability
  • Right to Algorithmic Due Process
  • Right to Fair Compensation

What the DDFA Does

The DDFA ensures transparency, regulates profiling, enforces strong data security, mandates data broker registration, and establishes equitable economic participation when personal data is monetized.

Why It Matters

Minnesotans deserve digital self-determination. The DDFA protects vulnerable communities, restores trust, reduces exploitation, and ensures individuals benefit from the economic value their data creates.
